About Bocchi The Rock

Bocchi the Rock! is a Japanese four-panel manga series written and illustrated by Aki Hamazi. It has been seriealized in Houbunsha's Manga Time Kirara Max magazine since December 2017. An anime television series adaptation by CloverWorks aired from October to December 2022.

Plot

Extremely anxious and socially awkward Hitori Gotō longs to become a rock musician in spite of her struggles, while fulfilling her desires to one day make friends. She is suddenly given a chance to do so after she is taken in by another girl, Nijika Ijichi, to become a member of her newly formed Kessoku Band.

Characters

Kessoku Band

Kessoku Band is the central band in the series, based in Starry, a live house in Shimokitazawa. The meaning of Kessoku Band is same meaning of zip tie.
